I am currently a post-doctoral researcher at [LadHyX](https://www.ladhyx.polytechnique.fr/fr/) (École Polytechnique) and [Physical Microfluidics and Bioengineering](https://research.pasteur.fr/en/team/physical-microfluidics-bioengineering/) (Institut Pasteur), where I am working with Profs. Charles Baroud and Gabriel Amselem. I am interested in microfluidics, soft matter, transport phenomena, and fundamentals of enzymatic kinetics.

 I obtained my PhD in Mechanical Engineering at Stanford University in June 2025, under the supervision of [Juan G. Santiago](https://microfluidics.stanford.edu/) and [Andrew D. Griffiths](https://www.lbc.espci.fr/home/). Prior to this, I obtained a Diplôme d'Ingénieur from CentraleSupélec and a M.S. in Mechanical Engineering from Stanford University in 2022. Aside from my research, I have been a Teaching Assistant at CentraleSupélec and Stanford University.
